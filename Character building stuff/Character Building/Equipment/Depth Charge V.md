# Depth Charge V
![[systems-pf2e-icons-default-icons-consumable.svg|150]]

**Ammunition** any

* * *

Carved with stylized images of water or aquatic life, depth charges that are fired underwater or at a submerged target function with their normal range increments and can hit no matter their normal damage type. This ammunition explodes if it hits a target underwater, dealing 11d6 bludgeoning damage in a 20 foot burst DC 32 fortitude according to its type. This burst doesn't extend out of the water.
