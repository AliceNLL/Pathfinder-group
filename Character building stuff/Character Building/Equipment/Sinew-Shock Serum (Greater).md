# Sinew-Shock Serum (Greater)
![[systems-pf2e-icons-equipment-alchemical-items-alchemical-elixirs-sinew-shock-serum.webp|150]]

**Activate** A Interact

* * *

This serum jolts the body to help shake off physical debilitations. When you drink this elixir, it attempts a counteract check with a 1d20+19 Counteract Modifier to remove the [[Clumsy 1|Clumsy]] or [[Enfeebled 1|Enfeebled]] condition from a single source, using the source of that condition to determine the counteract level and DC.
