# Wand of the Spider (2nd-Level Spell)
![[systems-pf2e-icons-equipment-wands-specialty-wands-wand-of-the-snowfields.webp|150]]

The handle of this wand is a 5-inch-long piece of rosewood, but most of its length is a pair of twisted spider legs covered in gossamer webbing.

**Activate** Cast a spell

**Frequency** Once per day, plus overcharge

* * *

**Effect** You cast _[[Web]]_ at 2nd level, but the strands of webbing are envenomed. Any creature that fails their Athletics check or Reflex save to navigate the web takes 1d6 poison{1d6 poison damage}.

**Craft Requirements** Supply a casting of _web_ at 2nd-level.
