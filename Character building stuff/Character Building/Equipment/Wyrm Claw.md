# Wyrm Claw
![[icons-creatures-claws-claw-hooked-purple.webp|150]]

The tip of a metallic dragon's claw is set into a band of silver. When you summon dragons using the claw, you must choose metallic dragons.

*   **Armor** After you cast a conjuration spell by activating the _wyrm claw_, you gain resistance 5 to acid, cold, electricity, and fire for the next minute.
*   **Weapon** After you cast a conjuration spell by activating the claw, you gain a Breath Weapon activity that takes 2 actions and deals 6d6 damage with a DC 28 Basic reflex. Choose the area and type of damage when you use Breath Weapon: a 30 foot line that deals acid, electricity, or fire, or a 15 foot cone that deals cold or fire. You can use this activity once before the end of your next turn.

6d6 acid 6d6 cold 6d6 electricity 6d6 fire
