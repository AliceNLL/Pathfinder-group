# Form Of The Bat
![[icons-sundries-books-book-red-exclamation.webp|150]]

**Frequency** once per hour

* * *

You can transform into a harmless bat. You gain the effects of a 4th-level _[[Pest Form]]_, except you always transform into a bat.
