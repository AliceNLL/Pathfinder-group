# Quaking Stomp
![[icons-sundries-books-book-red-exclamation.webp|150]]

**Frequency** once per 10 minutes

* * *

You stomp the ground with such force that it creates a minor earthquake, with the effects of the _[[Earthquake]]_ spell.
